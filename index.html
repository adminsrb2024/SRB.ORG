Who Can Donate?

Please ensure you meet the following criteria before donating.

You CAN Donate If:

  • You are between 18-60 years old.
  • Your weight is at least 45 kg.
  • Your hemoglobin level is above 12.5 g/dL.
  • You have not donated blood in the last 3 months.
  • Blood pressure and pulse rate are normal.

You CANNOT Donate If:

  • You have cold, flu, or fever.
  • You have recently had a tattoo or piercing (within 6 months).
  • You are pregnant or breastfeeding.
  • You have major diseases (HIV, Hepatitis, etc.).
